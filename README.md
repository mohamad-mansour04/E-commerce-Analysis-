📈 Key Insights
✅ Product Insights

Electronics = 60% of total revenue but has the lowest profit margin and highest return rate.
Beauty, Fashion, and Sports deliver 50–60% margins, showing strong pricing efficiency.
Laptops alone generate 0.29M in revenue, creating a dependency risk.

✅ Customer Insights

Home Office and Corporate customers drive the highest revenue and profit.
Consumer segment has the lowest revenue and lowest AOV.
Return rate is highest among Home Office customers.

✅ Regional Insights

Central and West regions perform best.
North and South underperform → opportunity for regional growth strategies.
Revenue shows seasonal peaks in Jan–Feb and December.


✅ Recommendations

Reduce Electronics return rate through quality checks and better descriptions.
Promote high‑margin categories (Beauty, Sports, Fashion) to diversify revenue.
Focus retention strategies on Home Office & Corporate customers.
Launch marketing efforts in underperforming regions.
Run mid‑year campaigns to reduce seasonal dips.
